Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

People like to ask who we are and why we do what we do. The answer to that is that we are just a group of car enthusiasts like you who wanted to make content easy for everyone to access.

Do we have to pay for anything?

Absolutely not! Our site is completely free for everyone to use!

Are your articles legit?

Our articles are 100% legit. All our articles are fact checked to ensure you are getting real news and not rumors.

How do I get my pictures on your site?

At the moment we don't have self posting feature but it is in the works. For the moment you can go to our contact page and submit a request to have your photos uploaded. Alternatively you can join our discord and submit your photo in there as we tend to choose from there.

How do I join the team?

We are actively looking for people who want to help make a positive impact in the community. You can join our discord and talk to a member of our staff to help you through the process.
