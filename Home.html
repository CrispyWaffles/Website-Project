Last Call Challenger

The end of an era

About

"The Challenger Final Call" is a limited-edition Dodge Challenger model that commemorates the end of an era for gas-powered muscle cars. Dodge announced this special edition in 2021 to commemorate the conclusion of production for the current generation of Challengers. Only 1,492 pieces of this limited edition model will be made, making it a highly sought-after collector's item among vehicle fans.

The 6.2-liter V8 engine in the Challenger Last Call delivers 797 horsepower and 707 pound-feet of torque. Its engine, along with an eight-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive, allows the Challenger to accelerate from zero to sixty miles per hour in 3.4 seconds. The Last Call also has a slew of performance enhancements, such as an updated suspension system, Brembo brakes, and a lightweight hood.

Aside from its excellent performance, the Challenger Last Call has a distinct design that distinguishes it from other Challenger models. It has a striking black and gold color scheme, with gold stripes on the bonnet and trunk and gold accents on the wheels and inside. The Last Call also has a special badge on the dashboard that indicates it is a limited edition. The Challenger Last Call is expected to be a sought-after collector's item among muscle car lovers for years to come, thanks to its powerful engine, unusual design, and limited manufacturing.
